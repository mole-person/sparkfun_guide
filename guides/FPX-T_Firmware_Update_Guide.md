# FPX-T Firmware Update Guide

This guide will document how to check and update FPX-T firmware.

Sources: [FPX-T Firmware Update](../docs/sparkpnt-fpx-t-firmware-update.html), [SparkFun RTK Everywhere Introduction](../docs/sparkfun-rtk-everywhere-introduction.html)

## Goals

- Check the installed firmware version.
- Understand when an update is worth doing.
- Back up settings if supported.
- Update firmware without losing critical configuration.
- Confirm the receiver still works after the update.

## Update Safety Checklist

- Do not start an update with a low battery.
- Do not interrupt power during the update.
- Record the current firmware version before updating.
- Record important settings before updating.
- After updating, confirm Bluetooth/BLE, WiFi configuration, GNSS position, and correction settings.
