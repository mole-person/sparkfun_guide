                              Pay only for what you use

                              Say goodbye to the rigid high annual service fees of typical RTK suppliers, and say hello to pay-as-you-go, subscription or hourly-based plans, by-device and pooled plans. Uniquely flexible usage-based plans allow you to pay only for what you use, lowering your operating costs.  

                              For example, growers using autonomous farming equipment may only need GNSS corrections during seeding and harvest. In this case, growers can simply activate PointPerfect Flex two months in spring and three months in fall and pay-as-you-go for only the months used. Aimed squarely at seasonal use cases like this, affordable, flexible service plans can save the farmer hundreds of dollars annually.

                              High accuracy and fast convergence

                              PointPerfect Flex is a PPP-RTK GNSS correction data service that delivers 3-6 centimeter¹ positioning accuracy with a convergence of just seconds. 

                              The service answers the fast-growing demand for precise positioning GNSS solutions including autonomous machines such as unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV), autonomous mobile robots, service delivery robots, heavy machinery automation, micro-mobility, and other advanced navigation applications. Emerging automotive applications include lane-accurate navigation and telematics.

                              Easy. Flexible. Seamless.

                              PointPerfect Flex is ready to use because it is pre-integrated with u-blox high-precision GNSS modules. Integration is easy and seamless with 3rd party high-precision GNSS RTK receiver modules or even with a mix in the same fleet. Industry standard protocols and data formats make it simple to get started right away.

                              PointPerfect Flex is natively supported by u-blox high-precision GNSS modules, providing a one-stop-shop solution from silicon-to-cloud.
                              PointPerfect Flex also works seamlessly with non u-blox high-precision GNSS RTK receiver modules or even with a mix in the same fleet.

                              Delivery is made easy via NTRIP protocols. Industry-driven SPARTN data format is bandwidth-optimized to reduce slash your mobile data costs with data rates that are up to 90% lower than typical network RTK services. PointPerfect Flex also supports the standard RTCM data format, so you can get started right away without the need for integration expertise.

                              Superior coverage

                              PointPerfect Flex is delivered via mobile internet to any number of end-devices. The broadcast provides uniform coverage on a continental scale, including up to 12 nautical miles (roughly 22 kilometers) off coastlines. With 99.9% uptime availability, you can rely on PointPerfect Flex 24/7 for your most critical applications. The service is backed by our full warranty and expert support team. PointPerfect Flex is available via mobile internet, as well as via L-band in several Latin American countries. 

                              Intuitive platform and flexible price plans

                              PointPerfect Flex is delivered via our enterprise-grade cloud platform, utilizes auto-scaling technology, and is proven to support billions of messages. The intuitive interface provides a self-serve environment from which users have autonomy to manage IoT device fleets, manage billing, monitor events, and have complete API control of functionality. 

                              Without an automated mechanism, the provisioning process involves installing the device credentials at manufacturing. This process is tedious and can slow down the production line, increasing manufacturing costs. Zero Touch Provisioning (ZTP) makes it easy to deploy huge device fleets, eliminating the headaches of managing digital certificates. ZTP enables devices to provision themselves automatically in the field, the first time they access PointPerfect Flex.
