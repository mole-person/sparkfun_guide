Welcome

This is the online product manual for the SparkPNT FPX-T; a high-performance, precision, MFi certified, all-band GNSS RTK combination rover and base station. If this is your first time using the FPX-T, follow check out our Quick Start Guide for initial instructions on setting up the FPX-T as a base station or begin surveying with your mobile device. Again, thank you for purchasing our SparkPNT FPX-T!
